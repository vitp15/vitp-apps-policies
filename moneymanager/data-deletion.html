Request Account and Data Deletion

If you want to delete your MoneyManager account and all associated data , please follow one of the methods below:

Method 1: In-App (Recommended)

Go to Settings > Delete Account and Data within the mobile app.

Method 2: Email Request

Send an email to vitp.work@gmail.com. from the email address associated with your account. Use the subject: Account and Data Deletion Request.

Note: Once processed, your Firebase authentication record and all data stored in Firestore will be permanently deleted. This action cannot be undone.
